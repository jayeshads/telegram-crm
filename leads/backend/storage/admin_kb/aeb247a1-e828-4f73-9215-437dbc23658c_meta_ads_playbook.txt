Meta Ads Playbook — Local Business Lead Generation

1. Always start with a Lead Generation objective for local service businesses.
2. Use a $15-25/day starting budget for testing phase across 3 ad sets.
3. Placements: prioritize Instagram Reels and Facebook Feed for awareness campaigns.
4. Use Advantage+ audience expansion once 50 conversions are logged.
5. Creative rotation: refresh primary creatives every 5-7 days to avoid fatigue.
6. For gyms and fitness studios, testimonial-style video ads outperform static images by 2.3x average CTR.
